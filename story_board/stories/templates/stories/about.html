Welcome!

Story Board is a platform for creating and publishing stories. A story is anything from an actual full length fictional story to the story of your day or anything else that you wish to publish. You can pushlish a story with a user account or you can create an anonymous story.

Create an Account:

With an account, you can publish a story with a by line, view all of your published stories on your profile page, recommend stories and follow other authors.

Publish without an Account:

Without an account, you can publish a story without a by line. You can view other author's profile pages but you can not follow them or recommend thier stories.
